# Compass

Status: v0.1 stub. Canonical source: [`../foundation/commonunity-architecture-v0.2.md`](../foundation/commonunity-architecture-v0.2.md), section *"Compass"*.

## What Compass is

Compass is the initial human-facilitated onboarding and source-data process. It is the **first threshold into CommonUnity** and establishes the person's foundational data, context, questions, and relationship to the system.

Compass is not an intake form or a diagnostic. It is the **first act of witnessing**.

## What Compass produces

By the end of a Compass process, the member has:

1. A relationship with their facilitator and (indirectly) the system steward.
2. Captured source-data sufficient to generate an Om Cipher: birthdate, Gene Keys, Human Design, name/gematria, seed syllable, and ideally a Bhramari tone capture.
3. The beginnings of a Living Profile: current projects, intentions, sankalpa, questions, creative stream.
4. An issued Om Cipher as **initiation seal** and entrance marker into Studio and cOMmons.

## Why it cannot be self-serve

Self-serve sign-up would skip the act of witnessing and would treat onboarding as data collection. CommonUnity is not a personality test or branding exercise. The facilitated process is the product.

This is a hard constraint, not a phase. A future "lighter" surface (e.g. `omcipher.com` standalone) may offer a self-serve sigil — but that is **not** entry to CommonUnity.
