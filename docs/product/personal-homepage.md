# Personal Home Page

Status: v0.1 stub. Canonical source: [`../foundation/commonunity-architecture-v0.2.md`](../foundation/commonunity-architecture-v0.2.md).

## What the Personal Home Page is

The Personal Home Page is the **face the member shows the world** — their public refraction of Om Cipher + Living Profile.

It is built inside Studio (via the homepage builder / Website Preview path) but lives outside Studio as a public surface.

In the member journey, the Personal Home Page sits between Studio and cOMmons:

```text
Studio → Personal Home Page → cOMmons / Field
```

## The four compass points

The Personal Home Page is organised around CommonUnity's four compass points (see `commonunity-context.md` at repo root for the full methodology):

- **The Work** — Law of Awareness
- **The Lens** — Law of Clarity
- **The Field** — Law of Balance
- **The Call** — Law of Creation

Each is anchored in a universal law. Together they form a complete map of the member's living identity — what they do, how they see, what sustains them, and what they are here to create.

The page is written in **first person**, in the member's own register. It reads like a real person, not a personal brand.

## Where the Om Cipher appears

The Personal Home Page renders the member's Om Cipher in `full` mode via the canonical engine. The cipher acts as the visual signature of the page.

## What the Personal Home Page is not

- It is not a marketing landing page.
- It is not a CV.
- It is not generated by AI from scratch — its content is shaped from Compass session material, in the member's own language.
