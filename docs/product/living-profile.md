# Living Profile

Status: v0.1 stub. Canonical source: [`../foundation/commonunity-architecture-v0.2.md`](../foundation/commonunity-architecture-v0.2.md), section *"Living Profile"*.

## What the Living Profile is

The Living Profile is the **subjective / lived qualia layer** of a member. If Om Cipher is the fixed source pattern, the Living Profile is **lived becoming**.

It captures what the person has done, is doing, and will do with their foundational pattern: projects, offerings, questions, intentions, work, creative stream, relational commitments, and the evolving meaning of their life.

## Relationship to Om Cipher

```text
Om Cipher       = source pattern
Living Profile  = lived expression
```

They are two sides of the same coin and are generated together through Compass. The Living Profile is **not** a Studio artifact; Studio is where the member *works with* the Living Profile, not where it is born.

## What it contains (open shape)

The Living Profile is intentionally open in structure. Typical material:

- Current projects, offerings, and services
- Active sankalpa / intentions / questions
- Creative stream entries
- Relational commitments and ecology
- Practice, healing, and learning threads
- Public bio fragments
- Evolving sense of one's Work, Lens, Field, and Call (per the CommonUnity four-compass-point methodology — see `commonunity-context.md` at repo root)

## How it shows up

- In Studio, the Living Profile is the member's living workspace and the input to many Studio tools.
- On the Personal Home Page, the Living Profile is refracted into the public face the member shows the world.
- On cOMmons, the Living Profile feeds the public projection layer — never directly, always as a member-controlled refraction.
