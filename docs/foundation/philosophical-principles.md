# Philosophical Principles

Status: v0.1 stub. Canonical source: [`commonunity-architecture-v0.2.md`](./commonunity-architecture-v0.2.md).

This document names the orienting commitments that hold CommonUnity together. When a design or implementation decision is unclear, these principles are the tie-breakers.

## 1. A new baseline of creation, oriented toward Unity

CommonUnity is not a productivity tool, a personality test, or a social network. It is a developmental ecosystem for helping a person translate their source pattern into lived creation, public expression, and relational unity.

Every feature should be evaluable against the question: *does this raise the baseline of creation toward Unity, or does it merely add capability?*

## 2. Witnessing before tooling

The first act of CommonUnity is witnessing — through facilitated Compass. Software grows out of relationship and source-data work, not the other way around. Members do not enter through a self-serve software funnel.

Implication: invitation-led onboarding is non-negotiable. Self-serve sign-up is not a feature to add later; it is a different product.

## 3. Source pattern and lived expression are two sides of one coin

- **Om Cipher** is the fixed/objective source pattern: birthdate, digital root, Gene Keys, Human Design, name/gematria, seed syllable, Bhramari resonance.
- **Living Profile** is the subjective/lived qualia: projects, offerings, questions, intentions, the evolving meaning of a life.

Neither is more real than the other. They are generated together through Compass and inform every downstream layer.

## 4. Resonance over directory

cOMmons defaults to intention-led resonance: a member offers a sankalpa, need, question, or offering, and the Field returns resonant presences or pathways. People are not browseable cards. Discovery is mediated by intention, not by scrolling.

## 5. Embodied input over symbolic input

The Bhramari / Humming Bee capture is a somatic offering — the person sounds themselves into the system. This is treated as a ritual and resonance signature, not as biometric security. Embodied inputs are preferred over purely symbolic ones wherever the design allows.

## 6. One canonical engine

Om Cipher must be rendered by a single canonical engine module, used identically across Studio, cOMmons cover, public profile, and any future standalone product. Visual divergence between surfaces is a bug, not a feature.

See [`../foundation/om-cipher.md`](./om-cipher.md) and [`../product/om-cipher.md`](../product/om-cipher.md).

## 7. Warm dark-field aesthetic discipline

The visual language uses radial gradients, opacity gradients, and warm dark-field color discipline. It must never feel neon or arbitrary. Color comes from frequency through octave equivalence; geometry comes from source pattern.

## 8. Credit-conscious, targeted work

Avoid broad audits. Prefer small, targeted implementation passes with explicit acceptance criteria. Use files and handoffs for long structured work rather than pasting everything inline. (See [`../handoffs/next-thread-handoff.md`](../handoffs/next-thread-handoff.md).)

## 9. Integrity is audited, not assumed

At each milestone, the system is audited against the [adapted 8 limbs of yoga](./adapted-8-limbs.md). This is not a code review — it is a coherence review: does the work, at this stage, still serve the seed pattern? See [`../governance/audit-rituals.md`](../governance/audit-rituals.md).

## 10. Sovereignty of the member

The member's source pattern and lived expression belong to them. Public projection layers exist (e.g. cOMmons public surface) but must always be a deliberate, member-controlled refraction of private source data — never a leakage.
