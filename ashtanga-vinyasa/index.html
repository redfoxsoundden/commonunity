ॐA · Ekam · 1 of 9
— Standing · Neutral
Samasthiti
Equal Standing
Stand at Samasthiti. Feet together, spine tall, Ujjayi breath established. Prepare to begin.
Alignment & Drishti
  • Feet together, big toes touching — weight evenly across all four corners of both feet
  • Spine long, tailbone descending — engage the thigh muscles, lift the kneecaps
  • Arms at sides, palms open — shoulders relaxed back and down, chest broad
  • Drishti: Nasagrai — soft gaze toward the tip of the nose, eyes relaxed
  • Establish Ujjayi breath here before moving — steady, even, nasal breathing

Urdhva Hastasana
Raised Arms · Arms Overhead
Ekam — Inhale, sweep arms overhead, palms joined, slight backbend. Gaze to the thumbs.
Alignment & Drishti
  • Sweep the arms up and join the palms overhead — inner arms beside the ears
  • Lift from the navel center, draw the shoulders down away from the ears
  • Gentle upper backbend — the thoracic spine opens, not the lumbar
  • Drishti: Angustha Madhye — gaze to the thumbs

Uttanasana
Standing Forward Fold
Dve — Exhale, fold forward, hands to the floor beside the feet, head toward the knees.
Alignment & Drishti
  • Fold deeply forward from the hips — palms flat on the floor beside the feet
  • Legs straight, kneecaps lifted — fold leads from chest, not from rounding the back
  • Head releases fully down toward shins — neck relaxed, crown heavy
  • Drishti: Nasagrai — gaze to nose tip

  • Drishti: Nasagrai or Broomadhye — nose tip or third eye

Downward-Facing Dog
